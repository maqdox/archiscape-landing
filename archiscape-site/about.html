Our History

How It All Started

JUNE 15, 2018

Archiscape's name is a blend of two worlds: architecture and landscape. It all started in 2018 with a simple favor. A friend of a friend needed her backyard transformed. We said yes, not knowing it would change everything.

We poured ourselves into it. Late nights, endless zoom calls, sketches torn up and started over. We weren't just designing a space — we were learning what it means to truly care about someone else's vision.

Then came the text:

"Sounds good! Thanks for the beautiful work! You should believe in what you do because it's stunning!"

That was it. That was the moment Archiscape stopped being an idea and became a purpose.
